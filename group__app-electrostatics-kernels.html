Coulomb potential for 1-dimensional electrostatics problem.

Fills matrix \( A \) with values

\[ A_{ij} = \frac{1}{r_{ij}}, \]

\( r_{ij} \) is a distance between \(i\)-th and \(j\)-th spatial points. No memory is allocated in this function!
